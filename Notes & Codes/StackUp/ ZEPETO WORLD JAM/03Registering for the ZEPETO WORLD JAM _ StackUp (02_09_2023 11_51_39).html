Introduction
Now that you have learned about what ZEPETO is and how to build a map of a ZEPETO World, take a look at the ZEPETO WORLD JAM event. Find out more about the event coming up in January 2023 and decide if you want to participate in this event.
This quest will bring you through the steps necessary to register for the ZEPETO WORLD JAM 2023.The ZEPETO WORLD JAM is organised by AngelHack. 

AngelHack is one of the worlds largest and most diverse developer ecosystems. Known as the pioneer of global developer hackathons, over the past 10 years, AngelHack has partnered with AWS, Dell Technologies, IBM, Web3 Foundation (Polkadot), Celo Foundation, and Algorand Foundation to run global hackathons and drive innovation; reaching a global community of more than 260,000 developers.

For technical help on the StackUp platform & quest-related questions, join our Discord, head to the #quest-help channel and quote (C49Q3) when asking your question.
Learning Outcomes
By the end of this quest, you will have:
  • Joined the ZEPETO Developers Discord server
  • Registered for the ZEPETO WORLD JAM
  • Read and understood the ZEPETO WORLD JAM guidelines

Quest Walkthrough
Step 1: Register for the ZEPETO WORLD JAM
Before going through the process of how to create a ZEPETO World, feel free to check out this link to learn about the upcoming ZEPETO WORLD JAM 2023. If creating a World on ZEPETO is a challenge you relish, you can follow the instructions to register by clicking here. 
Step 2: Finding Support on the ZEPETO Discord Server
In developing your World, you may encounter some difficulties and require help. One way to get help is through the ZEPETO Developers Discord Server, where helpful community members answer development-related questions. Join the ZEPETO Developers Discord server here. 
Once youve joined the Discord channel, take a full screenshot of the tech-questions channel, just like what you see in Expected Output. 

Your screenshot should show:
  • Your full screen, including your taskbar (for Windows) / dock (for MacOS)
  • Your Discord ID
  • The tech-questions channel
  • Make sure that all parts visible in Expected Output are also visible in your screenshot(s)!

Rename your screenshot C49_Q3_yourStackUpName.png where yourStackUpName is replaced with your StackUp profile name.
Expected output
Step 3: Complete the Quiz and Prepare Your Submission
After registering for the ZEPETO WORLD JAM, you will receive the ZEPETO WORLD JAM 2023 Participants Guide in your email to get started. As you read the guidelines about the ZEPETO WORLD JAM 2023, complete a quiz to demonstrate your knowledge needed to participate in the event.
  1. First, make sure to complete and submit the quiz! To pass, you will need to get 9 out of 10 questions correct!
  2. Then, do not forget to submit the quest as well!
  3. When submitting the quest, in the text submission field, please feel free to give us your feedback about this quest!

Some important points to take note of when completing the quiz:
  • When choosing MCQ options, be careful not to switch options too quickly, to avoid any issues where your latest answer is not saved due to network lag.
  • Your quiz result will only be shown after the quests end date.
  • The answers to the quiz will be uploaded along with the reviewers comments at the end of the campaign.
