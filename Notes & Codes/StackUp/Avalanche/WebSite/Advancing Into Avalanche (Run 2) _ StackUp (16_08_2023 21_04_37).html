Description
Welcome to Avalanche!
Avalanche is a blockchain platform that utilizes scaling capabilities and quick confirmation times through its Avalanche Consensus Protocol. Since Avalanche is EVM-compatible, it uses Solidity smart contracts to run decentralized applications (dApps) on its network. This also means that Avalanche is highly interoperable with a number of the largest decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystems in the Web3 world, making it a very attractive protocol to learn about.

In this campaign, well be taking a deep dive into the world of Avalanche. Well cover core concepts such as its Subnet technology as well as the development tools needed to build in this ecosystem. Well also be learning how to write smart contracts - from creating our own fungible tokens to minting our own NFTs. On successful completion of the campaign, Stackies will be able to create a GameFi dApp that utilizes NFTs and allows users to auction NFTs through a marketplace!

If you dont have any prior experience with Avalanche, fret not! This campaign has everything you need to get started. After youre done with the campaign, you can continue the adventure here.

Heres some things you can do to prepare for the campaign
Join Avalanches Discord - Join and engage with Avalanches community ahead of time! Note that your Discord account must be created more than 24 hours ago before youre able to join the server
Download Core Wallet - Install the Core wallet extension to your Google Chrome browser
Obtain Fuji (C-Chain) Testnet Tokens - Using your Core wallet (or any other EVM-compatible wallet), obtain three drips of testnet tokens from this faucet

Stackies whose quests from that campaign were rewarded will not be eligible for rewards in the corresponding quests for this campaign. Important Notice #2: To celebrate this campaign, Stackies who get Approved or Rewarded for their submissions for at least 7 of the quests in this campaign, will get 5000 bonus StackUp EXP points at the end of the campaign (after all the quest submission reviews are complete). So make sure to try your best to try out as many of the quests as possible! Note that the EXP points might take up to a month before they are added to your account.

Through the course of this campaign, if you have questions about any of the various quests, join our Discord, and head to the #avalanche-helpdesk forum channel.

Learning Outcomes
This campaign will give you a comprehensive learning experience that will prepare you for developing on the Avalanche Network. Here are the things that will be covered:
Part 1: Gaining Fundamentals & Learning About Key Avalanche Developer Tools
Release Date: 21 Apr 2023 (Friday)    Prize Pool: $13,710
  1. Understand the key features and consensus mechanism used in the Avalanche ecosytem
  2. Know where to find community support and guidance
  3. Understand the concepts of virtual machines and Subnets
  4. Interacting with AvalancheGo APIs
  5. Deploying a Subnet on your own local network
  6. Using the AvalancheJS Library to create and transfer an asset
  7. Understand Avalanche Warp Messaging and HyperSDK (new)

Part 2: Intermediate Avalanche Concepts
Release Date: 24 Apr 2023 (Monday)    Prize Pool: $8,110
  1. Introduction to smart contracts and basic Solidity
  2. Deploying smart contracts to the Avalanche Fuji Testnet as well as our local Subnet
  3. Introduction to ERC token standards
  4. Learning about the Hardhat development framework

Part 3: Capstone Project - Building a GameFi dApp
Release Date: 28 Apr 2023 (Friday)    Prize Pool: $8,180
  1. Introduction to NFTs
  2. Building a simple memory card game and minting of NFTs
  3. Building smart contracts that facilitate an auction marketplace of NFTs
  4. Building a frontend that allows users to interact with the marketplace created

By the end of this campaign, youll be equipped with the necessary skills to start developing on the Avalanche ecosystem!
